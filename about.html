Art as a Way of Living: Improving Art Step By Step

Illustrator graduated from Bachelor of Multimedia Computing in YPC International College

Challenges to keep up with new art ideas to blend in current trend

Since the booming of the digital era, trends have been changing rapidly especially in the NFT market. One must not only have to have the ability to adapt to the new trend to satisfy the audiences, but also the skills to produce a style to cater to the audiences' taste. One of the most challenging things to live on art career is to promote your product to social media, especially when creative jobs becomes more competitive due to high exposure rate via medias. Another challenge is to keep inventing new ideas while satisfying the audience at the same time.

Ways to Get Creative with Art Ideas to Get More Audience

5 important steps to get creativity.

  • Experiment and play with new software and equipment.
  • Look around what is happening around the environment and apply it in your piece.
  • Practice different type of techniques to create dynamic creations.
  • Don't be afraid to find more references while thinking about new ideas.
  • Just have fun and practice, practice, and practice!
